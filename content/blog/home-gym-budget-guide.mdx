## You Don't Need a $5,000 Gym

Every home gym tour on YouTube features $3,000 racks, $800 specialty bars, and enough plates to open a commercial gym. That's cool, but it's not necessary.

I built a fully functional home gym for **$487** that lets me squat, bench, deadlift, press, row, and do isolation work. Here's exactly what I bought and why.

## The Essential Shopping List

### 1. Adjustable Dumbbells — $349
**Bowflex SelectTech 552** — Replaces 15 pairs of dumbbells. This is the single most versatile piece of equipment you can buy. Covers everything from lateral raises to heavy rows.

### 2. Pull-Up Bar (Doorframe) — $30
**Yes4All Doorway Pull-Up Bar** — Chin-ups, pull-ups, hanging leg raises. Simple, effective, $30. No holes in the wall.

### 3. Adjustable Bench — $89
**FLYBIRD Adjustable Weight Bench** — Flat, incline, and decline positions. Holds up to 620 lbs. Folds flat for storage. An absolute steal at this price.

### 4. Resistance Bands — $19
**Fit Simplify Resistance Bands** (set of 5) — Add variable resistance to dumbbell exercises, use for warm-ups, or do dedicated band work. Incredibly versatile for the price.

**Total: $487**

## What This Setup Covers

With these four items, you can do:
- **Chest:** Dumbbell bench press (flat/incline), push-ups, flies
- **Back:** Pull-ups, dumbbell rows, band pull-aparts
- **Shoulders:** Overhead press, lateral raises, front raises
- **Arms:** Curls, tricep extensions, hammer curls
- **Legs:** Goblet squats, lunges, step-ups, band squats
- **Core:** Hanging leg raises, weighted sit-ups, planks

## What to Add Next (When Budget Allows)

1. **Barbell + plates** (~$400) — Opens up squats, deadlifts, bench press
2. **Squat rack** (~$300-700) — REP Fitness PR-1100 for budget, PR-4000 for premium
3. **Stall mat flooring** (~$45 per 4×6 mat) — Protects your floor and deadens sound

## The Bottom Line

Don't let perfect be the enemy of good. A $500 home gym beats a $50/month membership you don't use. Start small, train consistently, and upgrade when you outgrow your equipment.
